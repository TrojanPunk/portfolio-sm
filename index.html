About Me

Saloni Malviya

I am an aspiring aerospace engineer and a first-year master’s student at the University of Michigan. Throughout my journey, I have had the privilege of working on diverse aerospace projects. Driven by an insatiable curiosity, I am deeply passionate about control systems and continuously seek opportunities to expand my knowledge. Every experience—big or small—has shaped my path, teaching me invaluable lessons and igniting my passion for innovation in aerospace.The challenges I’ve faced have only strengthened my determination to make meaningful contributions to the field. My goal is to be an active participant in shaping the future of the aerospace industry, not just a bystander. I am committed to equipping myself with the skills and knowledge needed to drive impactful change.
